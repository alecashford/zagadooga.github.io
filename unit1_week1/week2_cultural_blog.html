Exercise 2
Movement. I associate movement, not with physical movement through space, but with the sense of continuous personal development. Something I have noticed over the years is that I am happiest when I feel like I am "going somewhere" in life and unhappiest when I feel like I am stagnating, or standing still. I use the word movement to mean this kind of sensation, the feeling of consistently moving towards a goal, no matter what it is, and no matter how slowly. As long as I feel like I'm moving, and not stagnating, I tend to be happy.
