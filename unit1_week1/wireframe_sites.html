My Second Attempt Website Critiques

BBC News
The problem this website solves is that of straight news distribution, with little editorialization. They cultivate a reputation as a global outlet of reputable news, which is why they have news coverage from all over the world. The challenge visually is to cram a lot of information on the front page so you can get the gist of what's happening in the world at a glance, but avoid making the page overly cluttered. I also added a map of the world, where you can visually select where you want to focus, instead of selecting from tabs.



The Atlantic
The problem this site solves is providing interesting commentary on culture and current events. The design challenges are similar to the BBC, but they tackle it with a slideshow instead of multiple boxes. In my design I chose to play up this aspect, giving the slideshow more slides and making it bigger in proportion to the viewing area.



Reddit
Reddit is a good place to find agregated content of whatever you're interested in. Because there are so many subreddits, however, it is challenging to find the ones that actually appeal to you. I would change this by making a larger "random" and "favorites" buttons, making them more prominant in the experience. If possible I would also add a "recommended" section, but since that is not a part of the website currently, I did not add it.
