Each US state flag is imbued with history, but some of them are better designed than others. A good flag should be immediately recognizable at a glance at any distance. It should also be recognizable when viewed straight on or hanging limp on its pole. For this reason, flags that have overly-detailed graphics or a reliance on text are not considered well-designed flags. The flags of the US states are a good set to learn about flag design with, because they include a range of well and poorly designed flags. Here is my semi-subjective ranking of them:
